111% Amazing Fairy Tale Photo Shoot in Prague!

by Tommy Pemberton

Every little girl imagines herself in a fairy tale at some age. Be part of the imagined legendary deeds of The Baroness in Prague!

  • Launched: Apr 29, 2013
  • Funding ends: May 29, 2013
  • Remind me

Help us tell the story of imagined legendary deeds of The Baroness and make our main character's fairy tale come true in a storybook of her own.

Our characters are; The Baroness, The Baroness as a child, and a few magical characters to give the story some mystery. As in all fairy tales, a dash of magic and a sprinkle of drama will take place for The Baroness to find her fairy tale ending.

The photo shoot will take place in Prague and surrounding areas. Our main character has a family history dating back to the 14th century in this region, so it is the natural location for our story to take place. This sounds like part of the fairy tale, but it is actually the background of our real life main character and is a huge inspiration in the storybook.

This is a personal project for me, Tommy Pemberton. My goal is to tell the imagined tale of The Baroness and to give our main character a fairy tale storybook with her as the star. We will also share this story and the behind the scenes short film with the wonderful people who help us with this Kickstarter.

A gallery opening with an exhibition of large prints from the storybook will be held after the storybook is finished. This exhibit will be open to the public, and it is our hope that everybody who participates in the Kickstarter will be at the opening so we can give our thanks in person.

A message from our main character: " This project is dedicated to everybody who chooses to find happiness in life!"

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Our goal for this Kickstarter is to primarily cover the cost of air travel, and this is what our pledge goal is based on. You might also notice we have rewards that exceed our base goal. It is our hope to create a larger production budget and to share this project with a larger audience.
