It will be AWESOME!

                                                                 I love to meditate

I do it at least once every day - and more if I can find the time. But it wasn't always like that.

My interest in spiritual pursuits began when I was still a teenager. I began my career as a massage therapist & energy healer when I turned 20. I was into Yoga, Tai-Chi and a number of other practices.

I liked the idea of meditation, but it was sooo boring! 

It took me 20 years to discover that there are many different ways to meditate, and some are actually fun and exciting. For example: There are aerobic meditations you can do while you exercise; water meditations you can do while swimming; interactive meditations to do w/ your partner or friend; even hugging a tree can be a profound meditation!

That's what this book is all about. I want to bring the joy & benefits of meditation to those who think it is too boring, too time consuming or too weird. 

Ok, so maybe some of the meditations are a little strange, but they are fun. And they can all be done in 10 minutes or less (each). 

Illustrated by Melissa Redmond, talented artist and owner of Muse Gallery Studio 622 in Cedarburg, Wi 

Each meditation will have a one page description and a beautiful illustration or photograph.

This is a fun, light-hearted book that makes it easy to try out a few different meditations and discover the magic!

The book is AWESOME in itself, but it is only the beginning of the dream!

I plan to use this as a starting point to bring meditation to the world. 

People are struggling and suffering more than ever, and meditation can help in many ways, so I plan to broaden my meditation classes, not just in the Yoga studios, but also the workplaces and ultimately public & private schools.

Could you imagine learning how to meditate at 6 yrs old? or 10, 13 or 16? 

The truth is, my 15 yr old daughter is not impressed, although her friends think it's super cool (she wishes she had a "normal" dad). But my 10 year old son meditates and does yoga w me almost every day. He loves it and is one of the happiest, healthiest kids I know!

Peter:  I've been meditating for 27 years. At first, it was not so exciting, but over the years, I've come to really love it. Beyond that, I have come to rely on meditation to cope with stress, increase my joy and solve problems.

Also I believe that it is my calling in life to help people connect with themselves and Spirit more deeply.

When you back this project, I will make it flourish in a way that will make you proud to say “I helped make this happen!”

Melissa: I’ve loved drawing and books ever since I was a kid, so helping create a meditation book is a dream come true.  In my art, I try to create work that speaks truthfully of human experience.  With this collaboration, you will be taking part in making a very special book – beautiful, engaging, and impactful.

WHAT MAKES THIS BOOK AWESOME?

A few things:

IF YOU LIKE MEDITATION:

You will LOVE this book. It gives a fresh view on age old practices. Even seasoned meditators will appreciate ways to bring new life and perspectives to their practice. It's also a very fun & easy way to share your joy with others in your life.

IF YOU HATE MEDITATION BUT STILL HAVE TO READ IT:

·YOU WON'T HATE IT ANYMORE.  Honest!  Even the most hardened spiritual grinches will be able to find at least one or two meditations that you won't hate.

Meditation is like food. There are so many different types and ways to prepare it. If liver was the first food you ever tried, you might think you HATE food, but I promise that there is something yummy here that you will love.

IF YOU LIKE THE POTENTIAL OF MEDITATION AND HAVE BEEN FRUSTRATED IN THE PAST:

Yes, I hear you. That was my story for 20 yrs.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

The main challenge, once we are successfully funded, is the main challenge that authors have faced since the beginning of books: Having the time, space and creative inspiration to sit down and put it in writing.

In a way, this will be like a meditation manual. I know all of the meditations, so it is not so difficult to describe exactly what to do. But it is more than just a technical manual. It is a guidebook to great frontier: Inner Space, the Self and the realm of energy & spirit.

So the real trick is to describe the indescribable in a fun and artistic way that will inspire and enable you to not just do the meditation "properly" but ultimately reach the intended destination: Inner peace, outer peace, feelings of happiness & joy, a tangible connection with that part of yourself that is pure unconditional love.
