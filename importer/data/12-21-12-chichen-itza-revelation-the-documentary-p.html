“What really happened on December 21, 2012, in Chichen Itza, Mexico?”

How it all started...

In 1996 I lived in the hills of St. Ann’s Bay, Jamaica, for three months. I became a vegetarian, a percussionist, and a self-taught theologian. The Rastafarians in Jamaica are considered Judeo-Christian and many believe in eternal life. Ever since my Jamaican experience...I consider myself a man of every religion. From 1996 to this day...I also believe in eternal life. Many ancient philosophies declare that humanity is entering ‘the golden age.’ Ancient gnosis reveals that the eternal realm will occur at a specific point in time, rather than from a divine being. This documentary will undoubtedly be a catalyst into the golden age.

If this is the beginning, let us (all)...begin!

My name is Matthias, and this documentary will enable me to unify the world, bring peace & love to all nations, and wake the ones who have fallen asleep. The Great Spirit sent more than 2,000 beautiful beings and myself to Chichen Itza, Mexico for the 12/21/12 alignment, and I have returned with video footage that will enlighten and reveal inner wisdom to those who view it.

It all started in 2007, when I asked my University of Denver, Graduate Research and Writing professor if I may write a research paper concerning the 2012 phenomena. Being a Mormon, she immediately agreed and enlightened me about a Mormon belief concerning Christ being in the Western hemisphere after his resurrection. 

Mormons are intrigued by the history of the Maya, she said, because of an ancient Mayan leader known as, Kukulcan, who was also the leader of the Aztec, under his first name, Quetzalcoatl. Ironically, Quetzalcoatl/Kukulcan was quoted as being a "white bearded god.” She concluded, “Thus in Mormon circles, he is often identified with Christ.”

Thereafter, I wrote an extensive, 20 page research paper entitled, “The Maya Prophecy of 2012.” The book I chose to research blew my mind, “The Mystery of 2012: Predictions, Prophecies and Possibilities” by Gregg Braden, Daniel Pinchbeck, John Major Jenkins and many other enlightened authors. I read every chapter like a detective breaking open an ancient case. 

It was Jay Weidner’s chapter on the ‘Hypersphere’ that influenced me to create a television series at my community television station. My thesis project was soon approaching, and I knew that my TV series would play a major part of my project...“2012 A.D.” was born.

I later shortened the title to, “2012.” And yes, this was long before the Hollywood movie “2012!” 

View a summation of “2012” & various LaRasVision media projects at: http://www.larasvision.com/media-projects.html

I am a reggae artist, percussionist, and a DJ, thus, after a successful season of “2012,” my following TV series “2012 - The Scene Series” aimed to highlight my local music scene (Indie & Punk Rock, DJs & Hippy music)! If a transformation was to occur on 12/21/12, I knew that music and unity were the keys to the future.

My third television series, “HYBR1D” focused on electronic dance music spanning from Arizona to Florida, and Alabama to New Orleans. 

View a summation of “2012 - The Scene Series” and “HYBR1D” at: http://www.larasvision.com/media-projects.html

After years of studying and presenting information on the 2012 phenomenon, including the Maya, Inca, Hopi, Aztec, Hindu, and the like...I truly believe that one can create one’s future! I ended up in Chichen Itza, Mexico during the 12/21/12 alignment without planning to do so; I was supposed to travel to Thailand!! Yet, the Great Spirit has an ancient plan concerning synchronization. 

Thus, Mexico was not only my destiny, it was OUR DESTINY (in Chichen Itza alone, way more than 2,000 people synchronized)! It was all part of Spirit’s divine plan. 

This documentary will aide in the great synchronization in which all of humanity will acknowledge (very soon) that we are ALL...the luminous ones, whom the Inca Indians prophesied. We are ALL the “Pachacuti,” as Pacha means "earth" or "time," and cuti means "to set things right."

We must begin the anticipated ‘transformer of the earth,’ and we will do so, simply, with love. A 26,000 year cycle has begun anew, so too must our human family begin a new cycle of luminosity ‘knowing the light of truth.’
