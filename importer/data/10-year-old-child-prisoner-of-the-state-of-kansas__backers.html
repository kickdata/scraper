REVISION / UPDATE:

Although this fundraising effort has not reached its goal, I will still self-publish the two books that I have been writing for several years now, both of which are nearing proof-reading stages, and are thankfully reflective of the deeper goals intended in writing these books.

I wish to thank the two people who did show support, and I also want to thank Kickstarter and the Kickstarter community of incredible, visionary and creative people who make this all happen for the global community. 

Keep up the good work, and thank you for this platform.

For further updates on my forthcoming books, visit BrandonStraun.com

Peace, Love, and prosperity to all.

-Brandon Straun
