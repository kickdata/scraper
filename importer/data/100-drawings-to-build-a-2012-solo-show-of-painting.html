100 drawings.... to build a 2012 solo show of paintings.

by Marlene Rye

Funded! This project successfully raised its funding goal on December 13, 2011.

I am making 100 pastel drawings that will be the basis of 10 oil paintings that will be in my solo show in Philadelphia in 2012.

  • Launched: Oct 14, 2011
  • Funding ended: Dec 13, 2011
  • Remind me

I am getting ready for my 4th solo show at Gross McCleaf Gallery in Philadelphia in November of 2012.  I have been a painter for 20 years and have always used the landscape as my inspiration.  For this show, I am planning on creating 100 pastel drawings, that I will then use as studies for 10 large oil paintings that will be on display for my solo show.

My work explores the landscape through the eyes of the child... "Return to a time when nature was more than a backdrop that you walked through or drove by, a time in your childhood where all the fairy tales had not been outgrown or explained away.  Imagination ran free, unacquainted with the adult world of ‘facts’.  Here was a world not yet fully defined, not yet labeled and categorized. Remember finding that magical tree where anything and everything was possible, that secret space where trees became circus performers, magicians, dancers, and all things fantastical.  It was a world so charged, so brimming with possibility that you could close your eyes and see the space through your whole body, with all your senses."

I am starting this body of work in a way that is in opposition to my normal process, as I do not usually do any kind of drawing or small study before making large paintings.  I am excited about challenging myself to make 100 drawings and to see what this push does to my paintings.

Please consider backing this project, as I take on a new direction in my work! My work can be seen at www.MarleneRye.com.  

Some drawings already completed for this project can be seen at... http://marlenerye.com/galleries/pastel-drawings

I started this body of drawings in January 2011 and I am half way through. To have time to make the paintings from the drawings for my solo show, my plan is to have the 100 drawings done in about 2 more months. This is a huge undertaking for me! What a great accomplishment it will be, to have 100 pastel drawings to work off of, as well as being able to show the drawings themselves, something I rarely do because of the additional cost of showing drawings. I need your help to reach my goal! I need money for more pastel paper, frames so that I can show these drawings professionally when I am done, and of course I need oil paint and canvas to make the 10 paintings. It costs me about $15,000 a year to make the work that I put out into the world. With this project, I am taking on the additional expense of getting my drawings framed, the cost of special pastel paper, and the pastels themselves. Any amount helps, and remember by backing me, you will be able to choose either an archival digital print, drawing or small painting in rice paper that is part of this body of work! What a great way to be a part of my next show!
