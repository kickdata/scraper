10 Year Songwriting Anniversary Golden Spun/Rachel Hays Album and Comic

by Rachel Hays

  • Home
  • Updates 4
  • Backers 31
  • Comments 5
  • World Music

Funded! This project successfully raised its funding goal on July 8, 2010.

  • Embed

After ten years of writing songs I am ready to make my first album and put it out with a beautiful comic merging my two loves forever.

  • Launched: May 27, 2010
  • Funding ended: Jul 8, 2010
  • Remind me

On my birthday, June 8th, I will have been writing and singing and performing for ten years. I have tortured high school friends with angst-y impromptu concerts, played in all kinds of bands, sang backup for others, and continuously made my own shifting music.

This year I finally recorded rough tracks on my computer for the first time and was able to fully arrange them. These songs are something that I would be proud to put out in the world. I am living in Korea until the end of July and I would like to record my music here in a studio (prices are reasonable,especially since its just me), have it mixed by friends and print CDs.

The other main thing that I am doing with myself is making comics. I want to make an accompanying comic booklet that is equal to the CD and be able to give the CD as a supplement for people that know my comics and the comic as a supplement to people that are interested in my music.

Basically I'd like to let people pre-order the album/comic and anything additional to that I will take as a bonus and a challenge to make it all more awesome.

I have samples of my work (both music and visual art) on my blog: http://rachelrahhays.tumblr.com/ and I have a myspace account http://www.myspace.com/goldenspun.
I'd love your support in any amount and I promise to make good.
