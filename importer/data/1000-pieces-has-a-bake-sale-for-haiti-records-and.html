ABOUT THE ALBUM

The debut "1,000 Pieces" album "Yellow & Black & Black" will be released on February 1st.



It will be a combination of styles that you have not heard before.



It was produced, engineered, and mixed by Ryan Wasoba (Exercise, Say Panther, So Many Dynamos) at Bird Cloud Studio in Illinois.



It was mastered by Alex at Yes, Master (Jimi Buffet, Lambchop, Brooke Waggoner) in Nashville, TN.



ABOUT THE FUNDING

Truman and I have already donated over $3,000 to this project.



We are asking you guys for about $800 more to cover the costs of:

Shipping the records to our house

Printing and manufacturing the CDs

Printing the special puzzle lyric insert that comes with the record and CD.

Printing the outside jackets of the Vinyls and Records

ABOUT THE CHARITY

Oxfam America is working to make a difference in Haiti, so we are helping them out!



We are donating 50% of all profits made from all sales of vinyls, CDs, cookies, and downloads directly to them for specified use in haitian relief.



The only costs we are recouping is our actual cost of production.



Our mixing, mastering, and engineering costs are our donation.



Once all of the vinyls, CDs, and downloads have been sold, we will have donated over $2,200



ABOUT THE COOKIES

Our cookies are made from scratch in our kitchen in Savannah, GA



We use organic and local ingredients whenever available



We are completely open to suggestions about what kind of cookies you want to see in your mailbox, we are up for anything!



We can make them vegan, or gluten free if needed (most varieties are vegan anyways!)
