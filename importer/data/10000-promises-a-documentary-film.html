10,000 Promises (A documentary film about homeless children)

by Jesse Lafferty

  • Home
  • Updates 0
  • Backers 2
  • Comments 1
  • Mc Keesport, PA
  • Journalism
  • Embed

A documentary film shown through the eyes of 4 homeless children and 2 young mothers living below the poverty line in America.

  • Launched: Feb 12, 2011
  • Funding ended: Mar 14, 2011
  • Remind me

Hi my name's Jesse, an award winning filmmaker living in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. I'm planning on returning to the city where I grew up to film the documentary, "10, 000 Promises". I'm giving 4 children and 2 young mothers cameras and allowing them to film their every day lives and take photos. They will shoot and edit there own mini-documentary film telling their OWN stories through their OWN eyes, uninterrupted and unedited! Allowing everyone to see the raw struggles that these kids and families go through... because I was one of these kids. I know what it's like going to school after Christmas vacation and lying about getting presents, feeling hunger pains, wearing torn hand-me-downs, etc. Do you know what it's like going to the cupboard hungry and seeing nothing to eat? Knowing your mom is in the other room crying...

I'm going to film in the city of McKeesport, Pennsylvania (where I grew up). Known to some as a 3rd world-city. I have several families ready to film their emotional struggle through their lives.

I need money to provide the families and children of "10, 000 Promises" with video cameras and laptops to shoot and edit their own short documentary. at the end of the project I would like them to keep the equipment and do a follow up film a year after "10,000 Promises" ends.

1 in 50 children in America are homeless. 1.5 million children are homeless each year. Of the 2.3 to 3.5 million Americans who are homeless each year, 34% are families. These numbers are likely to grow as the economic recession worsens and escalating housing foreclosures increase.
